The Grand History of Coffee

Introduction

Coffee, a beloved beverage worldwide, has a rich and fascinating history dating back centuries.

Origins in Ethiopia

Legend has it that coffee was discovered by a goat herder named Kaldi in ancient Ethiopia. He noticed his goats became energetic after eating berries from a certain tree.

The Spread to the Arabian Peninsula

From Ethiopia, coffee made its way to the Arabian Peninsula, where it was cultivated and traded. Yemen played a key role in popularizing coffee houses.

Coffee in Europe

Coffee arrived in Europe in the 17th century, initially met with skepticism but eventually embraced, leading to the rise of European coffee houses as centers of intellectual and social exchange.

Modern Coffee Culture

Today, coffee culture is diverse, with various brewing methods, specialized cafes, and a global industry.

  • Pour Over
  • French Press
  • Cold Brew
